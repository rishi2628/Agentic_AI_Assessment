ACME CORPORATION — IT SECURITY POLICY
Document:  Password Reset & Management Policy
Version:   2.3
Effective: January 15, 2025
Owner:     IT Security Team
Review:    Annually (next review: January 2026)
Classification: Internal

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. PURPOSE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
This policy defines the processes and requirements for creating,
managing, and resetting employee passwords across all ACME corporate
systems, ensuring security while minimising disruption to users.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2. SCOPE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Applies to all ACME employees, contractors, and third-party vendors
who access company-owned or company-managed systems.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3. PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.1 Complexity
   • Minimum length: 12 characters
   • Must contain: uppercase letter, lowercase letter, digit, and
     one special character (!@#$%^&*()_+-=)
   • Must NOT contain: username, full name, or common dictionary words

3.2 Expiry
   • Standard accounts: rotate every 90 days
   • Privileged / service accounts: rotate every 60 days
   • Users will receive reminder emails at 14 days and 3 days before
     expiry.

3.3 History
   • The last 10 passwords may NOT be reused.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
4. SELF-SERVICE PASSWORD RESET (Preferred Method)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
4.1 Steps
   1. Visit: https://portal.company.com/reset
   2. Enter your Employee ID (e.g. EMP-12345).
   3. Verify identity via one of:
        a. MFA authenticator app code, OR
        b. SMS one-time passcode (OTP) sent to registered mobile
   4. Create a new password meeting §3.1 requirements.
   5. Confirmation email sent to your corporate email address.

4.2 Requirements
   • Must be registered for MFA before using self-service.
   • Portal available 24 × 7, subject to scheduled maintenance windows
     (Sundays 02:00–04:00 UTC).

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
5. HELP DESK ASSISTED RESET
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
5.1 When to use
   • Unable to access the self-service portal.
   • MFA device lost/replaced and recovery codes unavailable.
   • Account locked due to repeated failed attempts (see §6).

5.2 How to contact
   • Phone:   ext 4357 (internal) / +1-800-555-4357 (external)
   • Email:   helpdesk@company.com
   • Portal:  https://help.company.com → "Password Reset Request"

5.3 Identity Verification (required before reset)
   • Employee ID + date of birth, OR
   • Manager name + last-4 of SSN (data verbally confirmed only —
     never transmitted via email)

5.4 SLA
   • Business hours (Mon–Fri 08:00–18:00 local): 2-hour response
   • After hours: 4-hour response (on-call engineer)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6. ACCOUNT LOCKOUT POLICY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   • Account locked after 5 consecutive failed login attempts.
   • Lockout duration: 30 minutes (auto-unlock) OR immediate unlock
     via self-service portal / help desk.
   • Repeated lockouts (>3 in 24 hours) escalated to the Security
     Operations Centre (SOC) for investigation.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
7. PROHIBITED PRACTICES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   • Sharing passwords with colleagues, managers, or IT staff.
   • Storing passwords in plain text (emails, spreadsheets, sticky notes).
   • Using the same password for corporate and personal accounts.
   • Requesting a password reset on behalf of another employee without
     their written consent and manager approval.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
8. ENFORCEMENT
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to
and including termination, in accordance with ACME's Code of Conduct.
